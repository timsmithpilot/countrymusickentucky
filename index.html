THE BAND

Meet the Brothers

Three by blood. One by the road. All country.

Band of Brothers is more than a name. Three grew up as family; the fourth earned his place one mile, one stage and one song at a time.

Jeremy

Bass • Electric Guitar

The low-end anchor with the versatility to move from a locked-in groove to an electric-guitar part without missing a beat.

Ben

Drums

The steady engine behind the band—keeping every shuffle, two-step and driving country rocker right where it belongs.

Matt

Lead Vocals • Guitar

The frontman with a strong country voice and an easy stage presence that keeps the room connected from the first song to the last. Matt also serves in the United States Marine Corps Reserve.

Tim

Electric Guitar • Fiddle • Vocals

Adding Telecaster bite, fiddle fire and harmony vocals—the extra colors that give the band its unmistakable country sound.
